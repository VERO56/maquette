BY MICKAEL ON OCTOBER 26,2006

It's 'Arial Black' and #FFF just like the title and the headers, however, if the person viewing the site removed 'Arial Black' then the font-weight:600 property will take care of bolding it as much as possible.

HOVER NEWS & QUOTES

BY MICKAEL ON OCOTBER 18,2006

Right under the navigation menu ti the right you will see a heading called Recent Updates. If you hover over the news items a short summary will pop up and allow you to click to get to another page. You can use this as a link list giving people a short description of what your link is about. I learned the technique from Stu at CSS Play. The original demo was to display a small photo gallery this way but I adapted it for text.
